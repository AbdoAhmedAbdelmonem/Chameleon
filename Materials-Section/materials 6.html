    Data Visualization

    Data Visualization is the study of techniques and tools for representing data graphically to uncover patterns, trends, and insights. It explores charts, graphs, dashboards, and interactive visualizations using tools like Tableau, D3.js, and Matplotlib

  • CXO

    Convex Optimization is the study of mathematical techniques for optimizing functions where the feasible region and objective function are convex. It explores methods such as gradient descent, interior-point algorithms, and duality theory.

    Data Computation and Analysis is the study of techniques and algorithms for processing, analyzing, and interpreting large datasets. It explores data structures, statistical methods, and computational tools to extract meaningful insights.
